B2B2C Channel Strategy — Employer Outreach

Omada's model means most members come through employers and health plans — not organic search. The Sr. Director must design outreach campaigns that work WITHIN the employer environment: email from HR, intranet posts, payroll stuffers, benefits fairs, and direct mail.

Highest Conversion
Personalized Outreach Letter

Direct mail or email from employer/plan — personalized to the member's specific condition risk. "You may be at risk for diabetes. Your employer covers Omada at no cost." Omada pioneered this approach.

High Volume
Email Nurture Sequences

Sophisticated multi-touch email campaigns for eligible employees who haven't enrolled. Personalized by condition, demographics, and employer. A/B tested continuously for subject lines, CTAs, and timing.

Growing Fast
GLP-1 Digital Campaigns

Paid social and search for GLP-1 Support program — the fastest-growing Omada offering. Targets employees and plan members who are on or considering GLP-1 medications. High intent, high LTV.

Re-Engagement
Lapsed Member Campaigns

Targeted re-enrollment campaigns for previously enrolled but inactive members. Uses outcome data — "Members who complete the program lose an avg of 4.7% body weight. Come back." High ROI given no acquisition cost.

New Channel
Health System Referral Flow

Provider-referred enrollment path — patient receives referral from their physician or care team to enroll in Omada. Clinical credibility drives highest enrollment rates and best outcomes.

Open Enrollment
Benefits Season Blitz

Concentrated campaign during annual benefits enrollment — Oct/Nov for most employers. When employees are already thinking about health benefits, Omada enrollment spikes. Plan the blitz 90 days in advance.

Email Marketing — The Core Engine

From the job posting: "Drive sophisticated email marketing and, where appropriate, direct mailer and digital campaigns." Email is Omada's highest-ROI outreach channel — personalized, condition-specific, and scalable across all 1,800+ employer client populations.

SEQUENCE TYPE
Initial Outreach Series

6-email series for newly eligible members. Personalized by condition risk (A1C, BMI, BP readings from claims data). Week 1: benefit intro. Week 2: social proof. Week 3: urgency + ease of enrollment.

SEQUENCE TYPE
Open Enrollment Blitz

High-frequency series during Oct–Dec open enrollment window. 10-touch campaign over 8 weeks. Employer co-branded. Includes "last chance to enroll at no cost" urgency messaging.

SEQUENCE TYPE
GLP-1 Medication Trigger

Triggered campaign when claims data shows member started GLP-1 medication. "You're on Ozempic. Omada helps you build the habits that make it work long-term." Highest-converting new sequence.

SEQUENCE TYPE
Win-Back / Re-enrollment

Lapsed member re-engagement series. Personalized with their previous progress data. "You lost 8 lbs. in your first month. The program still works — and it's still covered." Uses own data against churn.

SEQUENCE TYPE
Member Milestone Series

Automated in-program engagement emails. Week 4 weight loss milestone. First A1C check. Coach anniversary. Peer group join. Keeps members feeling seen and progressing — directly reduces churn.

SEQUENCE TYPE
Employer HR Toolkit Emails

Monthly email to HR/benefits contacts at all 1,800+ employer clients. Program updates, member success stories (de-identified), enrollment tips, and upcoming campaign materials ready to send.

GLP-1 Deep Dive — The $15B Opportunity

GLP-1 medications are the most significant development in chronic disease treatment in a generation. 15M+ Americans are projected to be on GLP-1s by 2030. Omada's GLP-1 Support program is positioned as the behavioral foundation that makes medications work long-term — and Omada published the clinical proof in 2025.

The Clinical Proof

Omada published 2025 study: GLP-1 + Omada coaching = more fat loss, preserved muscle mass, and improved mental health vs. GLP-1 alone. This is the #1 acquisition asset for the program.

The Market Signal

Every employer covering GLP-1s (now required by many large employers) needs a behavioral support program. Omada is the natural pairing — and growth marketing makes that pairing visible.

The Acquisition Strategy

Claims-triggered email within 7 days of GLP-1 Rx. Paid search on high-intent keywords. Prescriber referral pathway. Employer benefit communications. Benefits fair presence.

The Revenue Impact

GLP-1 members have the highest LTV of any Omada program — longer engagement, more touchpoints, highest clinical complexity. Driving enrollment in this program has outsized commercial impact.
